Simple Macaroni and Cheese

Quick, easy, and tasty macaroni and cheese dish. Fancy, designer mac and cheese often cost forty or fifty dollars to prepare when you have so many extoic and expensive cheeses, but they aren't always the best tasting. This simple recipe is cheap and tasty.

Ingredients for Macaroni and Cheese

  • 1 box of macaroni
  • 1/2 cup butter
  • 1/2 cup flour
  • 1/2 teaspoon salt
  • ground black pepper
  • 2 cups of milk
  • 2 cups of shredded Cheddar cheese
  1. Bring a pot of water to a boil.
  2. To make the roux, start by melting butter in a saucepan over medium heat. Add flour, salt, and pepper and stir until smooth.
  3. Add Cheese Macaroni.
